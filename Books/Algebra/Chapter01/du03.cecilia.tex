\subsection*{Dummit Exercise 1.3.16 (Cecilia)}

The number of $m$-cycles in $\text{Sym}(n)$ where $n \ge m$ is:

\[
  \frac{n!}{(n - m)! \cdot m}
\]

Every ordered selection of $m$ elements from $\{1, \ldots, n\}$ determines a cycle, giving $\frac{n!}{(n-m)!}$ ordered tuples. Each $m$-cycle can be written in $m$ ways due to cyclic shifts, so we divide by $m$.
